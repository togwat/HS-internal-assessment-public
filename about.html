Our purpose

Rubik's cubes have been around for a while, and we have pretty much mastered it every way possible. While it is good enough as a fun toy, it could be so much more. We wish to introduce new ways to challenge the rubik's cube, through the untapped potential that is its plain-coloured surface. Not only is the rubik's cube a puzzle, but with exotic materials and fabulous imagery, it can also be a charming accessory.

What we do

Every single one of our rubik's cube is made with a unique design. It could be unique patterns, images, and even materials. To make sure our designs are always novel, We sell each and every design only once. This gives way for even more creativity in future designs.
